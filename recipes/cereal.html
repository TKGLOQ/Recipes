Cereal, a good snack sometimes

Choco pillows is a good cereal sometimes. I say sometimes because it is often a bit too high in sugar, meaning perhaps 15-25g of sugar per 100g. My daily sugar intake may be 40g, but more ideally it would be around 30g.

Instructions

To create a bowl of cereal, you simply need:

  • Milk
  • A bowl
  • Choco pillow cereals

After you have gathered these items, you simply:

  1. Pour the cereal into the bowl
  2. Pour the milk into the bowl

And voila, you now have a good bowl of cereal.
